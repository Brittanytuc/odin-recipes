Lemonade

Description

Milk, Milk, Lemonade, around the corner? More lemonade.

Tale as old as time. Sour, refreshing, yummy, delicious and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  1. Juice the lemons.
  2. Pour lemon juice into water.
  3. Pour sugar into water.
  4. Stir until incorperated. Enjoy!
