Ants On A Log

Description

I know that this image shows traditional ants on a log. But I don't like celery or rasins.

Thus in this version there will be substitutions. The only constant is change.

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  1. Cut your apples into slices.
  2. Smear peanut butter onto the slices.
  3. Sprinkle chocolate chips on the peanut butter. Enjoy!
